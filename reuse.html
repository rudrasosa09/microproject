Quick fact

Recycling 1 ton of paper saves about 17 trees and 7,000 gallons of water.

Why Recycle & Reuse?

Saves resources, reduces pollution, minimizes landfill waste, and conserves energy used in manufacturing.

What can you recycle?

  • Paper & cardboard
  • Glass bottles & jars
  • Plastic bottles & containers
  • Aluminum & metal cans
  • E-waste items

Smart Recycling Tips

  • Clean and dry materials before recycling.
  • Reuse jars, containers, and bags whenever possible.
  • Segregate biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste.
  • Donate old electronics instead of throwing them.
  • Upcycle items to give them new purpose.
