Maximize Your Impact Now

Renewable Energy Switch

HIGH IMPACT

Switch your electricity provider to a 100% solar or wind energy plan. This is the single biggest move for homeowners or renters.

Eco-Friendly Commute

MEDIUM IMPACT

Walk, bike, carpool, or use public transport for your daily commute. If you must drive, consider an EV or hybrid.

Recycle & Reuse EVERYTHING

MEDIUM IMPACT

Diligently recycle paper, plastic, glass, and metals. Avoid fast fashion and buy second-hand goods wherever possible.
