Smart energy saving tips

  • Install solar panels for long-term savings.
  • Use LED bulbs and energy-efficient appliances.
  • Turn off unused devices to reduce energy waste.
  • Improve home insulation to maintain temperature.
  • Choose renewable-energy providers when possible.
