Quick fact

Choosing public transport or cycling instead of cars can cut CO₂ emissions by up to 70%.

Why eco‑friendly transport?

Lower emissions, reduced fuel cost, better air quality, and improved health through active travel options.

Common green transport modes

  • Electric vehicles (EVs)
  • Public transport (buses, metro, trams)
  • Cycling & walking
  • Carpooling & ride‑sharing
  • Hybrid and fuel‑efficient cars

Smart eco‑travel tips

  • Use public transport whenever possible.
  • Choose EVs or hybrids for long‑term savings.
  • Walk or cycle short distances.
  • Maintain tyre pressure for fuel efficiency.
  • Plan routes to avoid traffic congestion.
